This document describes the celebration of Gandhi Jayanthi and some famous quotes

Gandhi Jayanti is a national festival celebrated in India to mark the occasion of the birthday of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, who is unofficially called the "Father of the Nation". It is celebrated on 2nd October.
It is one of the four national holidays of the country. The United Nations General Assembly announced on 15 June 2007 that it adopted a resolution which declared that 2 October will be celebrated as the International Day of Non-Violence. It is celebrated every year.

Famous quotes of Bapu every student should know

On the occasion of his birth anniversary, given below are few traits every student should have, in the eyes of Gandhiji:

Learn as...

"Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever".

Be the change...

"Be the change that you want to see in the world."

Without action...

"Without action you aren't going anywhere".
